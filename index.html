FAQs

Why is it so slow?

There are several things that could be causing performance issues, such as:

- DEVICE: Cookedwater only supports computers, NOT phones or iPads. If you are on a phone or ipad, things might download, but most things will not actually open.

- LOW POWER MODE: Make sure you turn off low power mode, cookedwater is known to have issues with this

- RAM: Your computer might not have enough RAM. Many of these games are unreleased, and so we do not know the actual minimum RAM required to run it, but in general at least 64GB of DDR6 or higher will provide the best experience. If you don't have enough RAM, you can download more for free at downloadmoreram.com

- OPERATING SYSTEM: Cookedwater has the best performance on Google Chrome on the latest version of WinDows 11 Pro. Windows 11 Home and any version of Windows 10, as well as MacOS X Snow Leopard or later are supported as well (Although the Safari browser will probably not work). Linux might work, but will not have as good performance as Windows.

- STORAGE: For the best experience, we reccommend at least 2 petabytes and a Gen 6 SSD.

- GPU: Many of the things on Cookedwater are very graphics-intensive. Therefore, we reccommend for a GPU either an Intel HD 620 or a 5090, although anything else with at least 64GB of VRAM will probably work.
